Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 degree latitude). Because these measurements were taken during January, the southern hemisphere is considerably warmer than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year the data was gathered.
